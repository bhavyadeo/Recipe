Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Milk as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add eggs, butter, and milk to make batter.
  3. Pour into a greased baking tin.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  5. Cool and serve with frosting.

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
