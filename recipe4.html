Indian Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, fry onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add chicken pieces, cook until tender.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice.

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
