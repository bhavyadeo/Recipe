Fresh Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Mix in a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss well and serve fresh.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
