Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and egg, whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat butter in a pan.
  4. Pour batter, cook until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve with syrup or honey.

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
