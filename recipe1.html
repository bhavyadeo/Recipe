Classic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic (chopped)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce, sprinkle cheese, and serve.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
